Frequently Asked Questions
How does churn prediction work?
Our AI model analyzes customer data like tenure, monthly charges, contract type and support calls to predict the probability of a customer leaving. A score above 70% means high risk.
How do I add a new customer?
Go to Customers → Add Customer. Fill in the required fields (name, email, phone, plan, monthly charges) and click Save Customer.
How do I send an SMS alert?
Go to SMS Alerts from the sidebar. Select the target customer segment, type your message and click Send SMS. You can also schedule it for later.
How do I create a campaign?
Go to Campaigns, fill in the campaign name, target segment, type (SMS/Email), message and dates, then click Create Campaign.
How do I download reports?
Go to Reports page and click the Download button next to the report you need. You can also generate a custom report with a specific date range.
How do I change the theme?
Go to Settings → Appearance and click the Light, Dark or System button to switch themes. Your preference is saved automatically.
